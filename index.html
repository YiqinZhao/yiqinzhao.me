🦸‍♂️ About Me
M.S. in Computer Science at WPI

My name is Yiqin Zhao (赵一勤 in Chinese), I love my name since it is an important lesson for me: to work hard, to be diligent. Which comes from the meaning of the Chinese character 勤 (qin). Currently, I am a first-year Computer Science master student at Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI). I'm also a member of the CakeLab research group fortunately advised by Prof. Tian Guo. My research interests lie in the board area of mobile system, computer vision and computer graphics. Recently, I'm working on building new types of efficient mobile augmented reality (AR) systems that leverage the power of deep learning to support immersive AR user experience. Prior to WPI, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from Tianjin Normal University (TJNU), Tianjin, China in 2019. Besides my research, I love cooking a lot and play guitar sometime. I'm also a tech guy, a sci-fi guy. Check more of myself at here.
