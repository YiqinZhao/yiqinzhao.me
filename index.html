🦸🏻‍♂️ About Me

🔊 My name pounces as: Yi-Chin

🪧 I'm currently interning at GoogleARVR for the 2022 summer, hosted by Sean Fanello.

I am a third-year Computer Science M.S./Ph.D. student at Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI) and a proud member of The Cake Lab research group. I feel extremely fortunate to be advised by my kind and wise advisor Prof. Tian Guo. I'm interested in designing system supports and optimizations for Augmented Reality (AR) applications. My recent research has a strong focus on designing system support for AR environment lighting understanding and photorealistic rendering.

Prior to WPI, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from Tianjin Normal University (TJNU), Tianjin, China in 2019. During my undergraduate study, I worked with Prof. Ziping Zhao on audio signal processing, speech emotion recognition, and affective computing. I was also a software engineer intern at Baidu during the summer time in 2018. In spring 2022, I interned in the Y-tech Graphics AI team at Kuaishou, mentored by Haibin Huang and Chongyang Ma.
