🦸🏻‍♂️ About Me

I am a second-year Computer Science M.S./Ph.D. student at Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI) and a proud member of The Cake Lab research group. I feel extremely fortunate to be advised by my kind and wise advisor Prof. Tian Guo. I'm interested in designing system supports and optimizations for computer vision and graphics applications. My recent projects have a strong focus on improving environment lighting estimation for mobile AR applications. (Check my latest open source project PointAR).

Prior to WPI, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from Tianjin Normal University (TJNU), Tianjin, China in 2019. During my undergraduate study, I worked with Prof. Ziping Zhao on audio signal processing, speech emotion recognition, and affective computing. I also did an internship in software engineering at Baidu. Apart from my research, I love cooking a lot and I also play guitar (check my Instagram). Learn more about me at here.
