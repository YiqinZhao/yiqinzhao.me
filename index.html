🦸‍♂️ About Me
M.S. in Computer Science at WPI

My name is Yiqin Zhao (赵一勤 in Chinese), I love my name since it always tells me an important lesson: to work hard, to be diligent. Which is the meaning of the Chinese character 勤 (qin). I am a first-year Computer Science master student in Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI), I'm also a member of the CakeLab fortunately advised by Prof. Tian Guo. My research interests lie in the board area of mobile system, computer vision and computer graphics. Recently, I am working on building new types of efficient mobile augmented reality (AR) systems that leverage the power of deep learning to produce immersive AR user experience. Prior to WPI, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from Tianjin Normal University (TJNU) in 2019. I love everything about the computers, I'm also a tech guy, a sci-fi guy. Besides my research, I love cooking a lot and play guitar sometime.
